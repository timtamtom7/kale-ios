## Description

KaleMac is your personal kitchen companion — designed for home cooks who want to plan meals, track nutrition, and feed their household without the chaos.

### Features

- **Weekly Meal Planning** — Drag-and-drop meals onto a 7-day plan. Plan for one or everyone in the house.
- **Household Profiles** — Create individual profiles for each family member. Track their nutrition separately or together.
- **Smart Grocery Lists** — Auto-generated from your meal plan. Check off items as you shop. Powered by grocery delivery integration.
- **Recipe Library** — Browse, search, and save your favorite recipes. Get AI-powered suggestions based on what you have.
- **Nutrition Insights** — See daily and weekly macro breakdowns at a glance. Know when you're hitting your goals — or falling short.

### Who It's For

- Home cooks managing meal plans for 1–8 people
- Health-conscious eaters who want to track macros without the complexity
- Families who want to plan smarter, shop faster, and eat better

### Aesthetic

Fresh, clean, and appetizing. A kitchen aesthetic that feels like a sunny morning — warm greens, cream tones, and pops of color from real food. Light theme by default; full dark mode support.
